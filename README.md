# ⇄ The Contrast Break

**Recovery between deep-work blocks.**

A real break inverts the load your work put on you. Find the axis that got strained, then do its opposite. Flip two or three at once for genuine recovery instead of a surface pause.

👉 **[View the printable guide](https://borenw.github.io/Contrast-Break-Guide/)** (Letter · Portrait)

## The Table

| Axis | While working (the strain) | Break = the opposite |
|------|---------------------------|----------------------|
| 👀 **Eyes** | Fixed near focus on a bright screen | Look far away, out a window, or **close them** for 2 min |
| 🧍 **Posture** | Seated, still, slightly hunched | Stand up and **walk**, or lie flat and reset the spine |
| 💪 **Big muscles** | Idle — only fingers moving | **Stretch**, take stairs, quick bodyweight movement |
| 🎧 **Sound** | Silence, or low ambient hum | Add **music or a podcast** — switch the channel to audio |
| 💡 **Attention** | Narrow, effortful, single-threaded | Let the mind **wander** — diffuse, no goal |
| 📍 **Place** | Same desk, same four walls | Change rooms or step **outside** for natural light |

## Two Ready-Made Breaks

### 🔄 The Full Reset — `6/6 axes` ●●●●●●
Stand up, walk outside, eyes on the horizon, earbuds in with music. Hits every axis — posture, eyes, muscles, sound, place, attention — in one 10-minute loop.

### ⏱️ The Desk-Bound 2-Minute — `4/6 axes` ●●●●○○
Can't leave? Stand, close your eyes, roll the shoulders and neck, breathe slow. No screen, no goal. Flips eyes, posture, muscles, and attention without moving an inch.
